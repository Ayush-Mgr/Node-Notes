 ## Stages of Knowledge engineering are:
 1. Knowledge acquisition:  it is the process of acquiring knowledge from knowledge experts, who are experts in their domain of knowledge
	 1. Interview : interrogating and asking questions about their domain.
	 2. Observing on site : watching how do you do their task how they perform
	 3. Protocol analysis : finding some rules protocols that the domain expert is following while performing task.
	 4. Discussion : talking generally about their domain.
2. [[3. Knowledge representation using logic|Knowledge representation]] : it is the process of representing knowledge into logical form it has various types such as, [[1 . Propositional logic|propositional]], [[2.First-order logic and predicate calculus|predicate]], [[3. Knowledge representation using logic#^b18a94|frames]], [[3. Knowledge representation using logic#^a7e3fa|Semantic Networks]], [[3. Knowledge representation using logic#^42339a|scripts]] and [[3. Knowledge representation using logic#^4a88a4|rules]].
 3. Knowledge validation : it is the testing of the knowledge to measure how accurate together knowledge is by knowledge engineer of knowledge experts.
