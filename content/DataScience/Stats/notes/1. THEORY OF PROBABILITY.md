# Random experiments , OUTCOME ,EVENT
- the **experiment** means an act which could be repeated under some given condition
- __Random experiments__ are those acts whose results depends upon chance.
	-  rolling of dice 
	-  coin toss
	- drawing cards of deck 
- **outcome** is the result of the random experiment  ^2d0716
	- _Head_ as H and _tail_ as T
	- outcome of role of a dice would be any number 1234…
- **[[1.2 EVENTS|1.2 EVENTS]]** A collection or subset of possible outcomes having distinct or specific characteristics  
	
- 1. **Examples:**
    
    - Random experiment: **Rolling a Die**
        - Outcome: Rolling a 3.
        - Event: Rolling an even number (2, 4, or 6).   
            
    - Random experiment: **Flipping a Coin**
        - Outcome: Getting heads.
        - Event: Getting either heads or tails.
