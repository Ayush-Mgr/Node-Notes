## odds
- $Odds =\frac{A }{\bar{A}}$
- A = the featured event , winners
- ${\bar{A}}$ = the not featured event , losers 
## [[4.Classic Probability|Probability]]
- $Odds =\frac{A }{\bar{A}+A}$
- A = the featured event , winners
- ${\bar{A}}$ = the not featured event , total players
