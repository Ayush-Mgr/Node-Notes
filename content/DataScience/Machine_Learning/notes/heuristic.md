- It is a strategy (Rule of Thumb) which find us the shortest cost to get the goal state
- it does not give [[optimal solution]]
- the strategies differs according to the problem
- used when we want solutions quickly 
### Types of Heuristics:

1. **Admissible Heuristic**:
    
    - Always underestimates or exactly estimates the cost to reach the goal, ensuring optimal solutions in algorithms like [[A* algorithm]].
    - Example: Straight-line distance in pathfinding.
2. **Non-Admissible Heuristic**:
    
    - May overestimate the cost and isn't guaranteed to provide an optimal solution, but can still be useful in practice.
