## interquartile range 
-  interquartile range :
	- $Q_3 - Q_1$
-  middle 50 % of data 
## quartile deviation or semi interquartile range
- half of *inter quartile range*
- *[[0. MEASURES OF DISPERSION#^dd62a0|Absolute Measure]]*
 	$\text{quartile deviation: } \frac{Q3-Q1}{2}$

## Coefficient quartile deviation 
	 $\text{Coefficient quartile deviation: } \frac{\text{quartile deviation}}{\bar{X}}\times 100$
