
- *[[0. MEASURES OF DISPERSION#^dd62a0|Absolute Measure]]*
	**Formula:**
	-    $\sigma  = \sqrt{variance}$
- *[[0. MEASURES OF DISPERSION#^830a35|Relative measures]]*
	- $\text{Coefficient of } \sigma= \frac{\sigma}{\bar{X}} \times 100\%$
	-  coefficient of standard deviation could also be used to measure the differences in standard deviation of same series with large difference in their mean
- Properties:
	- S.D dose not dependent on origin it depends on the scale of origin 
	- S.D remains same if each observation  is increased or decreased by constant , $y=x-c$ 
		- $c$  is constant 
		- S.D of x = S.D of y
		- just changed the origin the scale remain the same 
	- if each observation is multiplied or divided by  constant S.D will be affected similarly, $y=x.c$ 
		-  S.D of y = |c|.S.D of x
		- changed the scale of data
