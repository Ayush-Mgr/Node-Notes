2.  $N$ = 32 (sum of ( f ) )
3.  $N/2$ = 16 
4.  now C.F grater than 16 ($N/2$) is ==25== 
5. $L$ is our lower value of Class Interval of ==25==
6. $i$ is the inter between Class Interval
7. $f$ is the ==12== frequency corresponding to 25
8. $C.F$ is our ==13== , lower than 25
9. ### Median formula:

$\text{Median} = L + \left( \frac{\frac{N}{2} - CF}{f} \right)×i$

Substitute the values:

$\text{Median} = 20 + \left( \frac{16 - 13}{12} \right) \times 10$
$\text{Median} = 20 + \left( \frac{3}{12} \right) \times 10$
$\text{Median} = 20 + (0.25) \times 10$
$\text{Median} = 20 + 2.5 = 22.5$
