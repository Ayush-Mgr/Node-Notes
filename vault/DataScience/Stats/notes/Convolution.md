### Convolution Formula (Discrete)

The convolution of two discrete functions \( f \) and \( g \) is given by:

$$
(f * g)[n] = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} f[k] \cdot g[n - k]
$$
This computes the weighted sum of function \( f \) shifted and scaled by \( g \).
