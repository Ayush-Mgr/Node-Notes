## sample space AND sample point
- The [[Data Science/maths/sets|set]] of The [[1. THEORY OF PROBABILITY#^2d0716|outcome of probability]] is also called _sample space (S)_ ^f4ff4c
	-  $S=\{e_1..e_n\}$
- any particular outcome or the element of sample space is called _sample point (e)_ ^70b6f1
- type of sample space 
	- **discrete:** it has [[1. Types of data categorical and numerical#^4616e8|discrete data type]] sample points
	-  **continuous:** it has [[1. Types of data categorical and numerical#^b412a2|Continuous data type]] sample points
## EVENT
[[1.2 EVENTS|Event]] is a set witch is [[Data Science/maths/sets|subset]] of sample space
- $E\subseteq S$
## Events derived by set operations
[[representation of  various probabilistic concepts]]
 1. $A\cup B$ : the notes probability of _occurrence of at least one_ of these event A or B, A or B [[2.Terminologies#^99598a|mutually exclusive]] event.
 2. $A \cap B$: probability of  occurring both event A and B _at same time_ or also called compound event / compound probability
 3. $\bar{A}$ : denotes the non-occurrence of event A or event complementary to A or  opposite to A
 4. A-B: all the sample points of event A which is not  similar to the sample point of event B $A \cap \bar{B}$
