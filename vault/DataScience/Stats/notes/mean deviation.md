# Grouped

- *[[0. MEASURES OF DISPERSION#^dd62a0|Absolute Measure]]*
	- formula : $$\text{Mean Deviation} = \frac{\sum fi \left| x_i - \bar{x} \right|}{n} 
$$
- $n$ is the number of data points,
- $x_i$​ is the $i$-th data point,
- $\bar{x}$ is the mean of the dataset,
- $\left| x_i - \bar{x} \right|$represents the absolute deviation of each data point from the [[Mean]].

-  *[[0. MEASURES OF DISPERSION#^830a35|Relative measures]]*
	coefficient OF mean deviation = $\frac{\text{Mean Deviation}}{Mean}*100$

## Un Grouped
$$\text{Mean Deviation} = \frac{\sum \left| x - \bar{x} \right|}{n} $$
-  $n$ is the number of data points,
- $x$​ is the data ,
- $\bar{x}$ is the mean of the dataset,
- $\left| x_i - \bar{x} \right|$represents the absolute deviation of each data point from the mean.
