- When data have different units, example, the amount of rainfall and yield of a certain year, the height and the weight of a group of children
## **[[4.Correlation]]**
- From such data if we calculate  the relationship between them or strength of association between this variables, it is called correlation
- The simple correlation is also called *linear correlation*

## **Regression**
- From such data if we predict the specific dependable  variable from another independent  variable it is called regression 
- The simple regression is also called *linear regression*
