- often used to calculate [[4.Correlation|Co-relation]] when there is not any precise measurement, or the observation cannot be measured at all 
- Formula:
	$$R=1-\frac{6\sum d^2}{n^3-1}$$
	- n = no of observation
	- d = (Rank of $x$ - rank of $y$)


- lies between -1 to +1

Substitute $∑d^2$ and $n=4$ into the Spearman formula to calculate $R$.
$$R=1-\frac{6\sum d^2}{n^3-1}$$
