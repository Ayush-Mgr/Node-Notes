# Buffets Rule of Thumb
## 1. Cash vs Depth 
- `cash and cash equivalents > Depth`
- shows solid financial position with high liquidity and low financial risk
## 2. Depth to Equity ratio 
- `Total Liabilities / Shareholders Equity`
- should be below 0.80 ,The ideal ratio can vary by industry
- shows the company relies more on equity financing than debt to fund its operations.
## 3. Preferred Stock
- having non is better 
- it reduces fixed dividend obligations, avoids potential dilution of common shareholders' equity, and provides greater flexibility in financial management.
## 4. Retained Earnings 
- `year 2 Retained Earnings / Year 1 retained earnings`
- should show growth  during recession periods  
- shows company is keeping money for growth
## 5. Treasury Stock
- company should have invested in treasury stocks 
- shows bought back stock from investors
