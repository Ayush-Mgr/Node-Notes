#finance 
**Key Details Shown**:

- **Issue Date**: The date when the bond is issued.
- **Maturity Date**: When the principal amount will be repaid.
- **Coupon Rate**: The fixed interest rate paid on the face value.
- **Yield at Issuance**: Reflects the return based on the coupon rate and price.
-  **Position in the Rating Scale**:
    
    - Ratings generally follow this order (from highest to lowest credit quality):
        - **AAA**: Highest creditworthiness (extremely low risk).
        - **AA+**: Very high creditworthiness.
        - **AA**: Very high creditworthiness, slightly lower than AA+.
        - **AA-**: Very high creditworthiness but closer to the A category.
    - Below AA, the ratings continue with A, BBB, BB, etc., each indicating progressively higher risk.
