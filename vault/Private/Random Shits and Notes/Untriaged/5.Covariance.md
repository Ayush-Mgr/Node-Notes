- Covariance shows whether there is a tendency for both variables of [[2.Bivariate data|bivariate]] data to move together (positive covariance) or in opposite directions (negative covariance).
- it may be positive or negative or zero
- Formula
	$$cov(x,y)=\frac{1}{n}\sum(x-\bar{x})(y-\bar{y})$$
	- Simplified:
	$$cov(x,y)=\frac{\sum x y}{n}-(\frac{\sum x}{n})(\frac{\sum y}{n})$$
